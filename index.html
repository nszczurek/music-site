Sanah

Sanah is a Polish singer-songwriter who is quickly taking the world by storm. Born in Poland in 1995, Sanah has been creating music since she was a child. With her powerful voice and unique style, she has become one of the most popular singers in Poland. Sanah's music combines elements of pop, R&B, hip-hop, and electronic music to create a unique and exciting sound that her fans adore. Her lyrics are often inspiring and empowering, touching on themes of self-love, resilience, and acceptance. Sanah's Music Career Sanah first gained attention with the release of her debut album, “Killing Me”, in 2015. The album was a hit in Poland, reaching number two on the Polish music charts.
